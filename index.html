Standing out from the crowd.

Designed for performance and comfort.

With its sporty lines, narrow front grille and larger tyres there’s no mistaking this car is designed for complete performance. And with a brand new multi-link rear suspension it’s comfortable too.

Advanced safety as standard.

The new Honda Civic comes with Honda Sensing™ a group of advanced safety features designed to make your drive safer than ever. The safety features include Collision Mitigation Braking System, Lane-Keeping Assist and Road Departure Mitigation. It’s why the 2017 Honda Civic is one of the safest cars we’ve ever made.

Class leading boot space.

The boot of the new Civic has been completely redesigned to give you a massive 478-litres of boot space. That’s one more litre than the previous Civic and a whole lot more space compared to anything else in its class We’ve also widened the tail gate, making loading and unloading simple.
